Executive Summary

The AI Classroom Intelligence System (ACIS) is an enterprise-grade EdTech SaaS platform designed to solve the critical bottlenecks in modern educational assessment. By automating question generation through specialized Large Language Models (LLMs) and enforcing academic integrity via edge-based biometric analysis, ACIS empowers institutions to scale high-quality education globally.

The Challenge & Market Opportunity

Industry Challenges

  • Educator Burnout: Over 40% of faculty time is consumed by manual assessment generation and grading, detracting from actual teaching.
  • Academic Dishonesty: Remote and hybrid learning environments suffer from sophisticated cheating methods that traditional proctoring fails to catch.
  • Data Silos: Institutional leaders lack real-time predictive analytics to identify at-risk students before they fail.

The ACIS Solution

  • Generative Scaling: Autonomous, curriculum-aligned assessment creation in seconds.
  • Zero-Trust Proctoring: Advanced behavioral biometrics and gaze tracking that flag anomalies in real-time without latency.
  • Predictive Intelligence: Deep analytics engine forecasting cohort performance and identifying systemic learning gaps.

Platform Capabilities

AI Question Generation Engine

Dynamically generates context-aware, curriculum-aligned questions (MCQs, short answer, essays, and coding challenges). It utilizes fine-tuned LLMs based on uploaded syllabi, ensuring zero hallucination and strict adherence to Bloom's Taxonomy.

Zero-Latency Anti-Cheating System

Proprietary edge-AI algorithms monitor behavioral biometrics, micro-expressions, gaze deviation, and keystroke dynamics. The system employs browser lockdown and flagging mechanisms to ensure absolute integrity without requiring heavy cloud compute.

Adaptive Learning Engine

Modifies test difficulty in real-time based on Item Response Theory (IRT). ACIS dynamically adjusts to precisely gauge individual competence levels.

Predictive Analytics

Actionable insights into cohort performance. The engine identifies knowledge gaps across demographics and predicts failure rates weeks before mid-terms.

Gamified Leaderboards

Fosters healthy academic competition through anonymous, percentile-based ranking systems and mastery badges to drive student engagement.
