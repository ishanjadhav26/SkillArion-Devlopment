Inside the Lab

Research & Innovation Lab

SkillArion Development operates at the edge of applied science. Our ongoing R&D initiatives focus on breaking technological barriers in affective computing for non-human subjects.

  • Animal Emotion Modeling Developing supervised datasets correlating physiological spikes with documented behavioral responses.
  • Audio Foundation Models Training large-scale acoustic neural networks to classify sub-vocal frequencies and micro-barks.
  • Veterinary AI & Predictive Health Algorithmic early-warning systems for chronic pain and medical distress detection.
  • Edge AI Wearables Miniaturizing inference engines to run locally on low-compute battery-powered hardware.
