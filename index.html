The Challenge

Animals speak.
We just can't listen.

There are over 900 million pet dogs worldwide. Every single one communicates distress, pain, happiness, and fear in ways that are invisible to us. Until now.

What if your dog could tell you…

01 "I'm scared right now."
02 "Something hurts."
03 "I need help immediately."
04 "I'm happy right now."
05 "Something is wrong with me."
The Solution

Animal Emotion Detection & Protection System

A proprietary smart collar that captures multi-modal biological signals — acoustic, physiological, and behavioral — and translates them into precise emotional states using our custom AI model.

Explore the full technology
Voice & Audio Capture
High-fidelity microphone arrays capture bark patterns, pitch, frequency, and acoustic signatures in real-time.
Biometric Sensing
Heart rate, SpO2, temperature, and motion data streams are captured via embedded IoT sensors.
Multimodal AI Fusion
Our CNN-LSTM model fuses acoustic and biometric streams to classify emotional states with high confidence.
Language Translation
Detected emotion matrices are converted into natural language insights via our NLP translation engine.
Human Notification
Owners and veterinarians receive real-time alerts, wellness reports, and behavioral insights via mobile.
