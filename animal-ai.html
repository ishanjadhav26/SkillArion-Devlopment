How It Works

Six layers of intelligence.

The system operates as an integrated pipeline — each layer purpose-built and optimized, feeding into the next with minimal latency and maximum fidelity.

See the architecture diagrams
Layer 1 — Smart Collar Hardware
INMP441 microphone, MAX30102 heart rate & SpO₂, DS18B20 temperature, MPU6050 motion sensor, GPS module, emergency SOS, and bone-conduction audio output — all on a Raspberry Pi 4 edge unit.
Layer 2 — Acoustic Feature Extraction
3-second bark recordings are segmented and processed through MFCC (40), delta features (40), chroma (12), spectral contrast (7), and RMS energy (1) — generating a 140×130×1 output tensor.
Layer 3 — Biometric Normalization
Continuous biometric streams (heart rate, SpO₂, temperature, activity, bark count) are baseline-normalized, scale-normalized, and anomaly-flagged before fusion.
Layer 4 — CNN-LSTM Emotion Model
Three Conv2D layers extract spatial audio features. An LSTM (64 units) with multi-head attention captures temporal patterns. A Softmax classifier outputs 5-class emotion probabilities.
Layer 5 — Multimodal Fusion Engine
Emotion class, confidence, and all biometric features are fused through a Random Forest / XGBoost ensemble layer for final wellness and pain scoring.
Layer 6 — Translation & Notification
Detected states are translated into natural language and delivered via the mobile owner app, veterinary portal, and optionally through the collar's TTS voice output module.

Technical Specification

Every component, considered.

🎙️
Acoustic Capture
INMP441 I2S digital microphone with 3-second sliding window capture. 60 dB SNR, flat frequency response. Bark segmentation with silence filtering.
❤️
Biometric Sensing
MAX30102 pulse oximeter (Heart Rate + SpO₂), DS18B20 temperature probe, MPU6050 6-axis IMU for activity and posture. 5 Hz continuous sampling.
🧮
Feature Engineering
Librosa-based pipeline: 40 MFCC + 40 delta coefficients + 12 chroma + 7 spectral contrast + 1 RMS energy. Output: 140×130×1 normalized tensor.
🧠
CNN-LSTM Architecture
3 × Conv2D layers (spatial) → LSTM 64 units with multi-head attention (temporal) → Softmax 5-class output. Trained on labeled dog vocalization datasets.
🌲
Fusion Layer
Random Forest or XGBoost ensemble fuses acoustic emotion features with normalized biometric streams for final health status, pain score, and anomaly flag outputs.
📡
Edge Deployment
TensorFlow Lite / ONNX model optimized for Raspberry Pi 4. Sub-200ms end-to-end inference latency. WiFi + cellular fallback for cloud sync.
☁️
Cloud Infrastructure
AWS IoT Core for MQTT device communication → S3 for raw audio archive → Timestream for time-series biometrics → EC2 FastAPI serving model predictions.
📱
Mobile & Portal
Owner app with real-time emotion alerts, wellness history, GPS tracking, and emergency SOS. Veterinary portal with clinical analytics and risk assessment dashboards.
🔊
Voice Output
gTTS / pyttsx3 text-to-speech module. The collar physically speaks detected emotions to humans nearby: "Your dog is showing signs of anxiety."
