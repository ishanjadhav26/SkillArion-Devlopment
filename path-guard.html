Path Guard is an AI-powered smart wearable system designed to assist visually impaired individuals through real-time navigation, object detection, voice guidance, OCR reading, medicine recognition, currency identification, and emergency safety intelligence.

Executive Summary

Millions of visually impaired individuals face daily challenges related to navigation, obstacle awareness, medicine identification, and financial independence.

Path Guard addresses these challenges through a unified AI-powered wearable platform. By combining Computer Vision, GPS Navigation, and Voice Assistance into a discreet smart glass form factor, we are redefining accessibility technology.

The Challenge

Visually impaired individuals face systemic barriers to independence that traditional mobility aids cannot solve.

Navigation & Obstacles

Constant risk of injury from dynamic environments and overhead obstacles that white canes cannot detect.

Financial Independence

Inability to easily identify currency notes, leading to reliance on others during financial transactions.

Healthcare & Medicine

Dangerous potential for medication errors due to identically shaped pill bottles and unreadable labels.

Environmental Awareness

Difficulty reading public signs, menus, and documents without dedicated Braille translations.

Emergency Situations

Lack of immediate, location-aware SOS signaling when disoriented or in danger.

Dependence on Others

A cumulative loss of autonomy that impacts mental health, education, and career opportunities.

Core Features

Path Guard integrates nine powerful systems into a single intuitive interface.

👁️

Real-Time Object Detection

Uses YOLOv8 and advanced Computer Vision to identify and announce surrounding objects instantly.

🛑

Obstacle Avoidance

Combines ultrasonic sensors and AI to detect drop-offs and overhead hazards beyond cane reach.

🎙️

Voice Guidance Assistant

Natural, conversational voice-based interaction for hands-free control and feedback.

📍

GPS Navigation

Turn-by-turn safe route guidance optimized for pedestrian safety and accessibility.

💵

Currency Recognition

Accurately identifies and announces currency denominations for confident financial transactions.

💊

Medicine Identification

Recognizes medicine packaging and reads dosage instructions to prevent critical errors.

📖

OCR Reading

Real-time text extraction to read books, street signs, menus, and printed documents aloud.

🚨

Emergency SOS

One-touch trigger to broadcast live location and audio alerts to designated emergency contacts.

🌐

Multi-Language Support

Full operational support in English, Hindi, and Telugu, promoting true regional inclusivity.
